Result For Your Skin







Cleanse:

Start and end your day with a gentle foaming cleanser designed to remove excess oil and impurities without drying out the skin. Look for formulas with salicylic acid or tea tree oil to help control oiliness.

Tone:

Use an alcohol-free toner to rebalance the skin's pH levels and tighten pores. Look for ingredients like witch hazel or niacinamide to help regulate sebum production and minimize shine.

Hydrate

Apply a lightweight, oil-free moisturizer to provide hydration without adding extra oiliness. Look for non-comedogenic formulas with hyaluronic acid or glycerin to keep the skin hydrated and balanced.

Protect:

During the day, apply a mattifying sunscreen with broad-spectrum protection to shield your skin from UV rays while controlling shine. Choose a non-greasy formula that won't clog pores or leave a greasy residue.

Treat:

Incorporate a weekly clay mask or exfoliating treatment to help absorb excess oil, unclog pores, and prevent breakouts. Use it in the evening to deep clean and refine the skin's texture without stripping away essential moisture.
