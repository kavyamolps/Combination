Result For Your Skin







Cleanse:

Start with a gentle foaming cleanser to remove excess oil and impurities from your skin. Look for a cleanser containing salicylic acid to help unclog pores and treat acne.

Tone:

Use an alcohol-free toner to balance the skin's pH levels and tighten pores. Look for toners with ingredients like witch hazel or tea tree oil to help control oil production and soothe acne-prone skin.

Treat:

Apply a lightweight acne treatment or serum containing ingredients like benzoyl peroxide or niacinamide to target acne-causing bacteria and reduce inflammation. Allow it to absorb fully before proceeding to the next step.

Moisturize:

Use an oil-free, non-comedogenic moisturizer to hydrate your skin without clogging pores. Choose a moisturizer with ingredients like hyaluronic acid or glycerin to maintain hydration levels without adding excess oil.

Protect:

Finish your routine by applying a broad-spectrum sunscreen with an SPF of 30 or higher, even if you're staying indoors. Look for oil-free or mattifying formulas to prevent excess shine and protect your skin from sun damage.
